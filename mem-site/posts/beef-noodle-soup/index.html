beef noodle soup

Monday, February 24, 2025

The Experience
this is my absolute favorite dish I've never made so far! it tops the braised pork belly over rice in terms of level of involvement and tediousness...

there was so much more prep required. i sliced the beef into finger width, diced 2 large onions, 1 tomato, 5 spring onions, ginger, lots of garlic, carrots and daikon radish.

the seasoning was also much more involved: soy sauce, oyster sauce, soy paste, rice wine, sugar, sesame oil, tomato paste, white pepper, etc

I left it to simmer for 6 hours

The Food
super proud of myself for this one! the flavors were very similar to what my mom makes :)
