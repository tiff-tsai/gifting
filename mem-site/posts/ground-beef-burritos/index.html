ground beef burritos

Wednesday, October 2, 2024

The Experience
My first time making burritos! got some taco seasoning from wegmans and got to work. i added ground beef, corn, tomatoes, rice, veggies, cheese, and guac!

The Food
it tasted too healthy. it felt more like a wrap than a heart-stopping sloppy burrito, which is a shame. i tried toasting the burritos but it was still not greasy enough. if I were to make it again, I think I need to go ham with the seasoning and use steaks instead of ground beef to add some fat to the burritos.
