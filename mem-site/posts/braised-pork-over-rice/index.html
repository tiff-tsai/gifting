braised pork over rice!

Friday, November 15, 2024

The Experience
This is the most involved dish I've made, surpassing the kimbap! just chopping the bork belly stripes into 1cm pieces took me half an hr due to the sheer volume of the dish I'm making. i seared the pork belly till golden brown and till the oil releases. then I added the aromatics and spice/ herb packs. left to simmer for hours, and added hard boiled eggs towards the end!

The Food
this is such a classic and iconic taiwanese dish, I'm so proud of myself for making it! it was super fragrant, the room smelled like braised pork belly for the week lol. it was one of the more intensive meals I've made, and nate really liked it!
