peppered pork chop & stir-fried udon

Wednesday, October 23, 2024

The Experience
comparing stirfried linguini vs udon, I prefer udon. reasons are 2 folds: not only do I prefer the texture of udon, udon is easier to prepare. when making noodles I have to boil in a separate pot first, which adds more time and dishes to clean. for udon, I usually just take it out of the freezer and thaw in water. once they've thawed, I toss them into my stirfry and season. it is pretty much done immediately!

The Food
seasoned my pork chops better this time, lots of pepper! the pepper got rid of that somewhat unpleasant pork taste, which I was very happy with
