The Experience
In my opinion, thin sliced beef is the most convenient protein because it thaws quickly and cooks quickly. i always buy lots of them when I'm home in NYC cuz its so much cheaper there compared to buying them from Princeton. i portion out the beef when Im back on campus and store them in the freezer. super convenient for when I want a quick impromptu meal!

The Food
I realized i really like adding fresh cucumbers to my meals. it brings a level of freshness that really balances out the more jarring flavors like marinated beef!
