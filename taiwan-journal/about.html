what is this website?

if you're reading this, you probably already know that i’m a hopelessly sentimental person who eagerly clings to any opportunity to reminisce. if you know that, then you probably also know the tragic irony: i have the memory of a goldfish. if it weren’t for my compulsive habit of photographing nearly everything—my breakfast, lunch, and dinner, the first ginkgo leaf i spot each year, every squirrel that ventures within six feet--i’d likely have forgotten half of my life.

so, in an attempt to hold onto my fading memories, i decided to document my travels to taiwan, the country i raised me for the first half of my life. the initial idea came from my trip to taiwan with my boyfriend in january 2025. we traveled a few cities on the west coast, and i wanted to document it. i later decided that i want this to be more than just a documentation of the past, but also something that’s living and can be added to in the future.

if you are reading this, then you partook in my taiwan travel adventures. click around and find memories we’ve made together!

goofily,
--tiffany
